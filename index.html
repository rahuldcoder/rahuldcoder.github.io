About Us

It was my childhood dream to open a dog kennel as I was having a German Shephard in my home and we were of almost same age. My doggo named JOBO was 1month elder to me. He was always there for me. He never ate food without me or I can say without taking the first bite of his food. He was very caring and protective and sometimes does not allow even my di maa to touch me. At the age of 16, I lost him as he was very sick and after doing al the possible medications and treatments, he left me behind. I buried him in delhi with another coffin by his side where I want my body or my ashes to be buried. Now at the age of 21 I have opened a Pet Boarding in Bangalore and want to open in Lucknow and Surat as well. I have made a company SSLK where the four letters has the name initials of my pets. So you all are welcome with your furry pets to experience a whole new world at my place WooF and Tails. We take care of the furry ones, play with them and feed them. We promise to deliver your pet without even a single tick. We clean the kennels everyday and/or after every pet checkout. You furry friend is safe with us. It would be a great vacation time for you furry friend and that too without leash. If you want to organise a birthday party for your furry friend, our place is just the best pace for you. We offer Pet day care facility, overnight stay facility, spa and bathing facility, grooming facility. We are professional setup and your furry friends are our first and foremost priority. We ensure to provide a safe, secure and happy environment. We immediately address and notify pet parent any health issues during the period of stay and take them to the best veterian available.
